FAQ

Got questions? We've got answers.

Everything you need to know about the Lala Wash Wallet.

Does my wallet balance expire? ▼

No, never. Your paid balance stays in your wallet until you use it — no deadlines, no conditions.

Do my FREE bonus credits expire? ▼

Founding bonus credits are valid for 90 days from your first load. Your paid balance never expires.

How long do my member perks last? ▼

Starter perks run for 30 days and Founder perks for 90 days from your load — same as your bonus credits, so everything in your Founder load is good for 3 months. Perks simply pause after that until your next ₱1,000+ load reactivates them. Your wallet balance is never touched.

Can my family use my wallet? ▼

Yes! One wallet works for the whole household. Anyone you authorize can drop off laundry and pay from your balance.

How do I load or reload? ▼

Pay cash or GCash at the counter and your balance updates instantly on our system. Minimum reload is ₱200, anytime.

What if I don't do laundry often? ▼

No pressure — your balance sits safely until you need it, kahit gaano katagal. It never expires. Start with ₱1,000 and see how it feels.
