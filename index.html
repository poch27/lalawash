FAQ

Got questions? We've got answers.

Everything you need to know about the Lala Wash Wallet.

Does my wallet balance expire? ▼

No. Your paid wallet balance remains available as long as your account has at least one transaction within a 12-month period.

Do my FREE bonus credits expire? ▼

Yes. Promotional bonus credits expire based on the active wallet promotion.

Can I reload anytime? ▼

Yes. Minimum reload is ₱200.

Will every reload renew my membership? ▼

No. Only qualifying Wallet Loads (₱1,000+) renew your membership.

What happens when my membership expires? ▼

You can continue using your remaining wallet balance. Membership perks such as Priority Wash Passes and Dash Espresso discounts are paused until you renew with a qualifying Wallet Load.
