# Lala Wash Membership Program Strategy Brief

## Project Overview

I own an existing laundry business called **Lala Wash**.

I currently have approximately **45 existing customers** with their
**mobile numbers and email addresses**.

My goal is to launch a **prepaid membership / wallet program** that
increases:

-   Customer retention
-   Repeat visits
-   Customer lifetime value
-   Predictable cash flow
-   Operational efficiency

I plan to reach out to my existing customers through SMS, Messenger,
Viber, and email to invite them to join.

This is **not** a cold audience. These customers have already
experienced our laundry service.

# Business Objectives

## Marketing Goals

-   Increase customer retention
-   Increase repeat visits
-   Improve customer lifetime value
-   Improve cash flow

## Operations Goals

I also want this program to reduce operational risk.

Today, staff handle cash throughout the day.

I want to reduce:

-   Cash shortages
-   Theft
-   Miscounting
-   Human error
-   Unauthorized discounts
-   Unrecorded transactions

Long-term, I want customers to prepay into a wallet so future
transactions become wallet deductions instead of cash payments.

This creates:

-   Cleaner audit trails
-   Better accountability
-   Less cash handling
-   Faster checkout

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Current MVP

I already use **SpinScale POS**, which includes a wallet feature.

For the MVP I **do not** want to build my own wallet.

Instead:

-   Customer joins the membership.
-   Customer loads money into their SpinScale wallet.
-   Staff deducts future transactions from that wallet.
-   The landing page and marketing campaign encourage customers to adopt
    this prepaid system.

Later, after validation, I may build my own wallet platform.

Execution speed is more important than custom software today.
