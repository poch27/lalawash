Wallet Terms & Conditions

Last updated: July 19, 2026

These terms cover the Lala Wash Wallet membership program, including wallet credits and member perks. By reserving a slot or loading your wallet, you agree to these terms. Simple lang ang goal namin: malinaw ang usapan, walang gulatan.

1. The Wallet

  • Wallet credits are store credit usable only for Lala Wash services (and stated partner perks). Credits are not cash — they cannot be withdrawn, transferred to another person's wallet, or exchanged for cash, in part or in full.
  • Paid balance never expires. No deadlines, no maintenance fees, no conditions.
  • Minimum load and reload is ₱200, payable in cash or GCash at the counter.
  • Your balance is viewable anytime through your private wallet link. Keep the link private — anyone holding it can view (but never spend) your balance. Ask any staff to re-send your link if you lose it.

2. Founding Member Offer

  • Limited to the first 25 confirmed founding members, until July 31, 2026, whichever comes first. Reservations are confirmed one by one; unconfirmed reservations are released to the next person.
  • The founding bonus (Starter: ₱100 on a ₱1,000 load · Founder: ₱300 on a ₱2,000 load) is granted once per member, on the first qualifying load of ₱1,000 or more. It is a one-time rate and will not be offered again.
  • Bonus credits expire 90 days after they are granted. Your paid balance is never affected by bonus expiry.
  • One founding slot per person. Ang isang household ay maaaring mag-share ng isang wallet (see Family Wallets), pero hindi puwedeng kumuha ng maraming founding slot ang iisang tao.

3. Member Perks

Perks are active for 30 days (Starter) or 90 days (Founder) from each load of ₱1,000 or more. When perks lapse, your balance is untouched — perks simply pause until your next ₱1,000+ load.

☕ Welcome drink — one (1) classic drink at Dash Espresso, claimable once per member, only after your first load of ₱1,000+. Claim it at the counter; staff will record the claim.

🎂 Birthday wash — one (1) free Wash & Fold during your birthday month, once per year. Requires: (a) at least 6 months of membership, (b) perks currently active, and (c) claimed within your birthday month. Hindi ito napapasa sa ibang buwan o ibang tao.

⏩ Priority access — member laundry is queued ahead of walk-ins while perks are active. Sa sobrang dami ng laundry sa peak days, priority means "una sa pila," not a guaranteed finish time.

10% off Dash Espresso drinks (Founder tier) — valid while perks are active; the member (or registered family member) must be present.

All perks are personal, have no cash value, and cannot be converted to wallet credits or discounts on other items.

4. Family Wallets & QR

  • One wallet may be shared by household members registered by the primary member. Each registered member gets their own QR code.
  • Any registered member's QR (or verified name) can charge the shared balance. The primary member is responsible for who they register.
  • Report a lost phone or compromised QR at the counter — we can deactivate a member's QR immediately.

5. Transactions & Corrections

  • Every load and deduction is recorded and visible in your private wallet link, usually within seconds. Check your link after every visit — if a transaction looks wrong or missing, tell us within 7 days and we will trace it. Every transaction in our system is permanently recorded and tied to the staff member who processed it.
  • Corrections are made by reversal entries (never by editing history), so your transaction record is always complete.

6. Fair Use

  • We may decline, reverse, or investigate transactions that appear fraudulent or abusive (e.g., fake reservations, perk farming, reselling credits).
  • Promos can't be combined with other discounts unless we say otherwise.
  • Accounts inactive for over 24 months may be archived; your paid balance is preserved and restored on request.

7. Privacy

We collect only what the program needs: name, mobile, optional email and birthday month, and your transaction history. We never sell or share your data with third parties. Your wallet link shows your data only to whoever holds the link.

8. Changes

We may update these terms as the program grows. Material changes will be posted on this page and announced by SMS. Changes never reduce a paid balance you already hold.
