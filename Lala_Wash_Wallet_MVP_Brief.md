# Frequently Asked Questions

### Does my wallet balance expire?

No.

Your paid wallet balance remains available as long as your account has
at least one transaction within a 12-month period.

### Do my FREE bonus credits expire?

Yes.

Promotional bonus credits expire based on the active wallet promotion.

### Can I reload anytime?

Yes.

Minimum reload is **₱200**.

### Will every reload renew my membership?

No.

Only qualifying Wallet Loads renew your membership.

### What happens when my membership expires?

You can continue using your remaining wallet balance.

Membership perks such as Priority Wash Passes and Dash Espresso
discounts are paused until you renew with a qualifying Wallet Load.

# Wallet Rules

-   Minimum Reload: ₱200
-   Wallet balance is managed by SpinScale POS.
-   Wallet balance does not expire while the account remains active
    (minimum one transaction every 12 months).
-   Google Sheets tracks membership status only.
